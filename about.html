About Us

Runner’s Rewards is a subscription-based loyalty programme that offers runners of all ability's exclusive discounts, giveaways, and future event opportunities. Accessible on both Android and Apple devices, our platform is designed to reward your passion for running, no matter the distance or discipline.

Created by runners, for runners, we bring over 40 years of combined experience across track and field, cross country, trail, and road running.


Craig Bridges - Co-founder, Runner's Rewards

Craig is a seasoned Software Engineer with over 20 years of commercial experience and a lifelong passion for running. He started running at the age of 18 in 2004 and has since participated in nearly 300 races, ranging from 200 meters to 30 kilometers.

Specialising in the 800 meters on the track, Craig also competes in the 1500 meters, 3000 meters, and cross-country events. While he occasionally takes part in road races like 5Ks and 10Ks, he now focuses more on shorter distances. A dedicated member of Team Bath Athletics Club, Craig trains regularly at Bath University and competed in the 800 meters and 1500 meters at the 2023 British Indoor Masters Championship.

His passion for running extends beyond competition. Through his YouTube channel, Swift Trails, Craig shares virtual treadmill running experiences recorded with his GoPro, inspiring runners worldwide to stay motivated and explore new routes from the comfort of their treadmills.

In 2019 Craig played a role in the INEOS 1:59 Challenge, where Eliud Kipchoge became the first person to run a sub-two-hour marathon. As part of a team of athletes, he tested pacing formations by running 400m intervals at sub-two-hour pace. Their efforts, filmed using drones and other tech, helped refine the 'Y' formation ultimately used during Kipchoge's historic run.

Rebecca Pierce - Co-founder, Runner's Rewards

Rebecca brings over a decade of professional experience as a Sports and Wellbeing Manager, with a strong background in the sport and fitness industry. She began running in her early teens and has since competed in more than 400 races, covering distances from 100 meters to half marathons.

Her passion for sport extends far beyond running. Rebecca has also trained and competed in football, judo, cross-country, and triathlon. She has proudly represented her county in cross-country, judo, and athletics and recently achieved the honour of representing Great Britain in triathlon within her age group.

On the track, Rebecca specialises in the 800 meters, 1500 meters, 3000 meters, and steeplechase. Her competitive spirit is matched by a deep commitment to coaching and community involvement. She regularly coaches middle- and long-distance runners with Team Bath Athletics Club and swimmers at Keynsham Swim Club. Rebecca has also volunteered as a coach in judo, football, and athletics at the local level, giving back to the sports that have shaped her life.

Her expertise has expanded into the fitness and rehabilitation field, where she is a qualified personal trainer and exercise referral instructor. Currently, she is further enhancing her skillset through massage therapy training.

Rebecca’s lifelong dedication to sport, coaching, and wellbeing reflects her belief in the power of competition and the importance of supporting others in achieving their goals.

Together, we are passionate about creating a platform that enhances your running experience with rewards tailored to the individual runner.
