Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions. If you can't find the answer you looking for, feel free to contact us.

1. How much does it cost?

Membership is just £5.99 a month.

2. How do I join?

You can join in less than 5 minutes using our new members registration page.

3. How do I cancel my subscription?

You will have a 14-day trial period beginning the day after you download the Runner's Rewards app onto your device. You can cancel your use of the app at any time during the 14-day trial period without charge. To cancel your subscription simply open the Runner's Rewards app and navigate to your profile, then tap Cancel Subscription. After the 14-day trial period a 12-month cycle will commence.

4. When will the monthly giveaways be drawn?

Monthly giveaways will be drawn on the last Friday of each month. Annual giveaways will be drawn in January - a precise date will be announced closer to the time.

5. What do I get for my subscription?

Members enjoy access to exclusive discounts from our ever increasing list of partners. You are also entered into monthly and annual giveaway prize draws. Access to one of the most comprehensive running event databases in the UK, covering virtually all races. Members also get curated running-related news and updates. For more information view our benefits page.
